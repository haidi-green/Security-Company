Our Founding

A Passion for Security, a Commitment to Peace of Mind

Berimbolo Security was born from a simple desire: to empower families and businesses with the security solutions they deserve. Our founder, Damon, witnessed firsthand the emotional and financial toll of security breaches. This sparked a passion to create a company that offered reliable, user-friendly security systems, coupled with exceptional customer service.

We began as a small, local operation, prioritizing personalized security consultations and tailored system design. Our dedication to building trust and exceeding expectations fueled our early growth.

Growth & Beyond

From Local Roots to Expanding Horizons

Our local roots blossomed as we embraced cutting-edge technology and expanded our reach. This dedication to innovation earned the trust of a growing clientele. Today, Berimbolo Security remains true to its core values. We strive to make security accessible, offering peace of mind that allows you to focus on what matters most.

As we look forward, continuous innovation ensures we stay ahead of evolving threats, always committed to exceptional security and unparalleled customer service.
